How does it work?

The application communicates with your vehicle through an OBD-II scanner,inbuilt maps and a dash-cam.
It computes the data over the week and provides a detailed driving summary to you and your insurance provider.
You receive or lose points based on this calculated data.
Whenever you renew your insurance, you can use these points and save money!!

Do I have to pay?

You don't need to pay to use the application.
However, you need to purchase a compatible OBD-II scanner and a dash-cam yourself or through your insurance provider.
However, this is a small investment when compared to the amount of money you can save at the end.

  • What's the catch?

What's the catch?

There is no catch.
This application promotes you to adopt better driving practices by earning you points which you can use to pay for insurance.
Moreover, it also informs the emergency services if you are involved in an accident, which can save your life.
This application also temporarily hides mobile notifications when you are driving, thus reducing distractions.

  • What's differential pricing?

Introducing Differential Pricing

Via differential pricing, your insurance company may charge you differently based on your profile score.
This means that the people next door, even though they might be of the same age as yours, may end up paying more because of their driving habits.

Driving Safety through our app!!

Thousands of people die every year in road accidents.
Our initiative originated at YorkUHacks 2018 and is a step towards making Canadian roads safer and decreasing distractions while driving.
It's a win-win situation for you as you save money, for insurance providers as they save money, and us because we are more safer on the roads.

Cautionary Alerts

The application alerts you audibly if you have your parking brake on, or if you're running low on fuel.
This causes lesser distraction as compared to looking at the speedometer icons.

Service Reminders

The application informs you if your car isn't running right, or if a service is due.

Traffic Alerts

With Google Maps integrated into the app, you can use all the features Google Maps has to offer.

Emergency Services

The application automatically contacts the emergency services if a collision is detected which can help save someone's life.

Blocked Notifications

Notifications are disabled temporarily so that the user is not distracted.However, urgent notifications are audibly spoken.

Cost Saving

You save money on fuel,repair and insurance costs if you drive safely by using this application.

FAQ's

Is it compulsory to use this app?
If you want to pay less for insurance, it is necessary that you drive with this application opened.
However, it's not required by law to use this app. The usage is totally optional.
Are there any hidden charges?
No, this application is completely free to use. However, your insurance provider may charge a service fee or a one-time fee.
Do I have to buy a membership?
No, there is no membership for this application.
What if I forget my phone at my house/someplace else?
If you forget your phone, you won't get any points for that driving session.You would also not lose any points as there is no distraction while driving.

    How does this app work?
  • You keep this app open while driving which is linked to your insurance account.
  • Your score is generated weekly which is sent to your insurance provider.
  • You either gain or lose points which can be eventually used to pay off insurance fees.
